Career Exploration

Explore Your Career Pathways

Career exploration is a vital step in shaping your professional journey. It involves researching industries, roles, and companies to find the best fit for your skills, interests, and values.

At UMSI, we provide resources and support to help you navigate this process effectively. From informational interviews to job shadowing opportunities, we encourage you to take proactive steps in discovering your ideal career path.

Steps for Effective Career Exploration

  1. Self-Assessment: Reflect on your strengths, interests, and values to identify potential career paths.
  2. Research: Use online resources, attend career fairs, and connect with professionals to learn about different industries and roles.
  3. Informational Interviews: Reach out to UMSI alumni and professionals for insights into their career journeys and advice.
  4. Job Shadowing: Arrange to shadow professionals in your fields of interest to gain firsthand experience.
  5. Utilize UMSI Resources: Leverage the UMSI Career Development Office for workshops, counseling, and networking events.
